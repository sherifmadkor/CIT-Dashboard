Privacy Policy

ATMUP Finance — QuickBooks Online Integration · Effective June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ATM Universal Processors LLC ("ATMUP", "we", "us") handles information in connection with ATMUP Finance, a private, internal-use integration between ATMUP's own QuickBooks Online account and ATMUP's internal financial-reporting tools.

1. Scope

ATMUP Finance is an internal application. It is not offered to the public and is used solely by authorized ATMUP personnel to report on ATMUP's own business financials. It connects only to ATMUP's own QuickBooks Online company.

2. Information we access

Through Intuit's QuickBooks Online Accounting API, and only after an authorized ATMUP administrator grants access via Intuit's OAuth 2.0 flow, the application reads accounting report data belonging to ATMUP, such as Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, and Accounts Receivable / Payable summaries. Access is limited to the read-only scope com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting.

3. How we use it

The data is used exclusively to generate ATMUP's internal management dashboards and reports. The application does not create, modify, or delete any records in QuickBooks Online.

4. How we store and protect it

  • Authorization tokens and the resulting report data are stored in ATMUP's private, access-controlled environment.
  • Access is restricted to authorized ATMUP finance staff.
  • We retain data only as long as needed for internal reporting and remove it on request or when the integration is disconnected.

5. Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share ATMUP's financial data with any third party. Data is processed only within ATMUP's own systems and the services required to operate the integration (Intuit and ATMUP's internal reporting platform).

6. Disconnecting

An ATMUP administrator may revoke the integration at any time from within QuickBooks Online (Apps → Connected apps) or by contacting us. On disconnection, the application stops accessing QuickBooks data.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy: sherif@atm-up.com, ATM Universal Processors LLC.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; the effective date above reflects the latest version.
