Disconnect QuickBooks Online

ATMUP Finance — operated by ATM Universal Processors LLC (ATMUP).

How to disconnect

You can revoke ATMUP Finance's access to your QuickBooks Online company at any time:

  1. In QuickBooks Online, go to Settings → Apps (or the Intuit My Apps page), find ATMUP Finance, and choose Disconnect.
  2. An authorized ATMUP administrator can also disconnect from within the internal ATMUP Finance tool.

What happens after you disconnect

  • The integration immediately stops pulling any data from QuickBooks Online.
  • The OAuth tokens are invalidated and no further API calls are made.
  • Because the app only reads report data and stores no QuickBooks records, there is nothing retained to delete; cached dashboard figures simply stop refreshing.
