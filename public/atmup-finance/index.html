ATMUP Finance — QuickBooks Online Integration

An internal financial-reporting integration operated by ATM Universal Processors LLC (ATMUP).

What this application does

ATMUP Finance is a private, internal-use integration that connects ATM Universal Processors LLC's own QuickBooks Online company to the company's internal Microsoft Power BI reporting environment. It is used by ATMUP's finance team to automate the company's own management reporting (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, and Accounts Receivable / Payable dashboards).

How it works

  • An authorized ATMUP administrator connects the company's QuickBooks Online account using Intuit's standard OAuth 2.0 authorization.
  • The application reads accounting report data only (e.g. the QuickBooks Profit & Loss and Balance Sheet reports) via the Intuit QuickBooks Online Accounting API.
  • That data refreshes a set of internal finance dashboards. No accounting records are created, edited, or deleted.

Data & access

  • Read-only. Scope: com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting (read).
  • Internal only. The integration is not offered to the public and accesses only ATMUP's own QuickBooks company.
  • Not sold or shared. Financial data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties.
