Connect / Reconnect QuickBooks Online

ATMUP Finance — operated by ATM Universal Processors LLC (ATMUP).

How to connect

ATMUP Finance is a private, internal-use integration. It connects only to ATM Universal Processors LLC's own QuickBooks Online company, and the connection is initiated by an authorized ATMUP administrator.

  1. An authorized ATMUP administrator starts the connection from the internal ATMUP Finance tool.
  2. Intuit's standard OAuth 2.0 consent screen opens; the administrator approves read-only access (scope com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting).
  3. The integration then refreshes ATMUP's internal Power BI finance dashboards from QuickBooks report data. No accounting records are created, edited, or deleted.

Reconnecting

If access needs to be renewed, the administrator repeats the same authorization step. There is no public self-service sign-up — the app is not offered to outside customers.
